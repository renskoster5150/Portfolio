The BreadBook

The BreadBook was a group project in which we had to include a single recipe, but make it look like an entire fleshed out app. We received a recipe for making sourdough bread, so we decided on a baking recipe app. In the app users can read recipes, share pictures of their creations, and ofcourse look at the creations made by other users.
