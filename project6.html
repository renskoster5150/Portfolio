TUF (The Underground Finder)

This is a design similar to tinder, where the user can match with other people. The purpose for this app is however, to find people based on music taste. People who listen to music, that is not very mainstream, often struggle with finding people with similar music taste to go to concerts with. This app's sole purpose is for people to make groups and go to these concerts together
